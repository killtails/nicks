Nick's Est. 1977 — Wicker Park

Nick's has deep roots in this neighborhood.

The building dates back to 1911, and over the years it's been home to a mix of dreams, regulars, and many great nights. What started as a neighborhood bar has grown into a Chicago institution — known for cold beer and great music.

We're still doing what we love: hosting live music, pouring beer, and keeping Wicker Park Chicago's late night garden.

There's always been a place for everyone here. Pull up a stool and stay awhile.
