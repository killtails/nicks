About Nick's

Nick's Est. 1977 — Wicker Park

Nick's has deep roots in this neighborhood. The building dates back to 1911, and over the years it's been home to dreams, regulars, and many great nights.

We're still doing what we love: live music, cold beer, and keeping Wicker Park Chicago's late night garden.
