About Nick's

Nick's Est. 1977 — Wicker Park

Nick's has deep roots in this neighborhood. The building dates back to 1911, and over the years it's been home to a mix of dreams, regulars, and a whole lot of great nights.

We're still doing what we love: hosting live music, pouring strong drinks, and keeping Wicker Park weird in the best way.
